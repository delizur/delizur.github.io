OASYS Project

Levelling system for a lunar greenhouse

Machine DesignSolid MechanicsFEACADThermal Analysis
CSA Logo

Currently working in a team of students under supervision of the Canadian Space Agency, to design a levelling system for their proposed OASYS lunar greenhouse. The OASYS initiative intends to enable sustainable food production for NASA’s Artemis Mission for lunar and Martian exploration. Our team has been tasked with designing a system for stabilizing and levelling the OASYS greenhouse that can withstand the lunar environment for a minimum of 6 weeks. The project involves CAD modelling, material selection (considering lunar temperatures, outgassing, and effects of lunar regolith), machine design of the necessary moving components, along with FEA and thermal analysis of the entire system.
